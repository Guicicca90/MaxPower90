Spent a decade in credit risk at a factoring company before teaching myself statistics and ML to automate the job. Now I build intelligent systems at the intersection of finance, urban data, and AI agents.

Father of an autistic child — which is why I also build [una-edu](https://github.com/MaxPower90/una-edu): an AI platform that generates personalized learning materials for autistic students.

### Background

- **2013–2019** — Risk & credit analyst at Cicor Factoring. Manual work, spreadsheets, gut feeling. Started questioning the process.
- **2019–2023** — Co-founded Duplify. Built DupliNet: automated factoring platform with XGBoost/Random Forest credit scoring. First real ML in production.
- **2023–present** — CTO at [bee6](https://bee6.com.br). Data architecture, AI pipelines, and systems that turn messy business data into decisions.
